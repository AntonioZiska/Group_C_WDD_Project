The Benefits of War

The main goal of this section is to highlight the benefits of war when it comes to technology creation. It is known that war brings innovation in different fields.


This project is focused on the peace and war aspects of the SDG16 and this section aims to elaborate the war and how it somehow leaded to technological advances.

The conflict between countries can offer innovation in medicine as briefly mentioned on the war main page. You may be asking how War can be somehow beneficial considering it's toxic and against human-rights characteristics. The answer is layed on historical facts, to demonstrate in practice, this website will be covering some technologies created during war times.

During World war II and Cold war some technological and medical innovations were needed for military reasons as well as show dominance agaisnt the enemy. To discover how war leaded to innovation besides it's harmful aspect to humanity and ecosystem, it will be listed in this page 3 technologies that originated from war conflicts.
