This site is dedicated to raising awareness about SDG Goal 16, which focuses on Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. It highlights the importance of creating peaceful societies, where justice is accessible to everyone, and institutions are fair, inclusive, and resilient. Central to this goal is understanding the complexities of both war and peace and how these forces have shaped our world throughout history.

War has caused immense suffering, leading to loss of life, displacement, and destruction across the world, and yet it has also sparked innovation. On the other hand, peace offers stability, growth, and justice, but it is fragile and requires constant effort to maintain.

This site will explore both the costs of war and the benefits of peace. We will look at the challenges and opportunities they present, and how building a world based on peace can help create a better future.
