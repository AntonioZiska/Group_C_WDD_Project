Seek for innovation and self-destruction

The main goal of this section is to mention how war can be harmful, damaging both human lives and ecosystem. Although, It is known the War brings Innovation in different fields, but with what cost?


This project is focused on the peace and war aspects of the SDG16 goal and aims to elaborate on the benefits and drawbacks of each.


The conflict between countries can offer innovation in medicine, during the world war II a huge effort on medical procedures such as surgeries, first-aid technics, blood transfusion and vaccines and It is a matter of fact the battlefield reworked the medicine we know and still makes a positive impact.

Although, this quick progress which normally would take decades was layed on a necessity, the warzone required grand effort to save lives, either soldiers and civillians were massively affected for the sake of the leaders ambitions, and the price of this rapid and unnatural improvement was paid with countless human lives.
